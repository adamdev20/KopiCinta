How to Play

Place your bet during the 5-second countdown. When countdown reaches zero, Spaceman will launch and the multiplier will start increasing. Cash out at any time to secure your winnings before it crashes!

Multiplier Probability

The probability of reaching higher multipliers decreases exponentially. Only 10% of games reach 2x, 1% reach 10x, and 0.1% reach 100x.

About Spaceman

Spaceman is a crash game where players bet during countdown and cash out during flight. The goal is to cash out before the multiplier crashes. Inspired by Pragmatic Play's popular game.
